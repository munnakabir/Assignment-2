About Us

Welcome to the new academic year at EUREKA SCIENCE ACADEMY. The administrative team and our teachers are excited about this academic year and we expect you will find that EUREKA SCIENCE ACADEMIC staff members are caring, competent, dedicated and willing to assist you. We are working very hard to provide best possible learning climate for students. We believe that education is a shared responsibility and that the successful operation of an academy depends on the cooperation of everyone concerned: students, parents, and staff. The mission of the EUREKA SCIENCE ACADEMY is to create a partnership among the members of this triad. Each of us is responsible for doing our to make our academy a place where we can work and play together in harmony. EUREKA SCIENCE ACADEMY is a reflection of us all. Our policies are intended to provide a safe and orderly environment that will be conducive to learning. Our faculty and staff look forward to sharing their expertise in academics, special programs and extracurricular activities. We encourage you to get to know our academy, its programs, activities and schedule. Become an active participant in our academy by getting involved in our classes and activities. On behalf of the entire EUREKA SCIENCE ACADEMY staff and community, best wishes for a great new academic year.
